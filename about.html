About Us

Our story, mission, and the people who make ACK St. Mark's Parish - Malaa what it is

VISION

To strengthen Anglican Church built on the foundation of the apostolic faith in Jesus Christ with the ability to equip all God's people to face the challenges of the new millennium.

MISSION

To bring all people into a living relationship with God through Jesus Christ, through preaching, teaching, healing and social transformation and enabling them to grow in faith and live life in its fullness.

Our Pillars

Scripture: The primary source of faith and the ultimate standard for doctrine.

Tradition: The living heritage of the church, including liturgy, creeds, and historical practices.

Reason: The use of intellect and experience, including insights from science and the arts, to interpret faith in contemporary contexts.
